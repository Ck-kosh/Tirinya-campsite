Why Our Guests Keep Returning

Natural Beauty

Located directly on the Escarpment,natural balcony," this escarpment overlooks the Kerio Valley and is famous globally as a premier paragliding destination..

Classic Campfire Culture

Gather under the stars every evening for stories, roasted marshmallows, and acoustic tunes around our central firepit.

Eco-Friendly
