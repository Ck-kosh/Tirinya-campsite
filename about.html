Our Story

Nurturing Wilderness, Comfort, & Connection

Tirinya Campsite was founded on a simple dream: to create a peaceful sanctuary where families, friends, and solo adventurers can escape modern noise and gather around the warmth of a crackling fire.

The Tirinya Journey

Crafted by Nature, Managed with Passion

Tirinya Campsite sits on a historic piece of land on the Escarpment of Rift valley. What started as a simple family orchard has transformed over the years into a thriving eco-camp, loved by local and international travelers alike.

We designed Tirinya to blend seamlessly into the environment. Our traditional timber bandas are constructed using sustainable local pine and Grass roofs, while our expansive lawns are carefully maintained to protect Tirinya's unique soil and Escarpment.

"At Tirinya, we do not inherit the earth from our ancestors; we borrow it from our children. Our goal is to leave this beautiful environment greener than we found it."

Our Foundation

The Principles We Stand By

Environmental Stewardship

Minimizing your ecological footprint while actively protecting and restoring local ecosystem.

Authentic Hospitality

As a family-run campsite, we welcome every traveler like kin. Warm fires, cozy blankets, and friendly chats are always on the house.

Community Upliftment

Over 80% of our staff are hired from local villages. We actively partner with community initiatives and purchase organic food from Tirinya farms.

Wilderness Preservation

We design our layout and facilities around ancient trees and natural pathways, ensuring wildlife like monkeys and birds can roam freely.
